        <h2 id="about-us">🌱 About Us</h2>

        Silvatica was born from the idea that festivals should feel like home —  
        a place where creativity, community, and sustainability thrive.  
        We’re a small crew of dreamers, makers, and nature lovers building something beautiful in the woods every summer.


        <h2 id="coc">📜 Code of Conduct</h2>

        At Silvatica, kindness is non-negotiable.

        - Respect each other’s space, identities, and boundaries.
        - No hate speech, harassment, or violence of any kind.
        - Consent is essential.
        - Treat the land with care. Pack it in, pack it out.
        - If you see something off, say something — our safety team is always nearby.

        This space is for everyone. Let’s keep it magical and safe. 💚
